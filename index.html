3

Quarterly progress presentations to the committee, ongoing assessment and control
5

Upon completion - submitting models as a request for educational innovation regulation
üîç Three Key Insights
1. Solutions Already Within the System: Often, solutions to innovation challenges already exist within the system itself. There's not always a need for new legislation or regulatory revolution - sometimes it's enough to rediscover, interpret creatively, and activate existing tools.

2. Protected and Bounded Space: Always work in a limited scope and time-bound framework - controlled experimentation that enables learning and risk management before scaling to the entire system.

3. Engage Partners from the Start: Always ensure connection and updates with relevant stakeholders. Engaging relevant regulators from the beginning can remove barriers down the road.
üí° Core Principle: RRI - Responsible Research and Innovation
A process that takes into account the actual and potential impact on the environment and society during research and development, from ethical, legal, and social aspects. Every project must demonstrate compliance with responsible innovation principles.
